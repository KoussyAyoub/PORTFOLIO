Job Search Website

This project forced me to develop a dynamic website offering job seekers a panel of job offers. Our website is characterized by the diversity of its functionalities as well as the ease felt during their manipulation.
This project also contains a second part of web scraping in which we managed to collect the necessary data from the internet.
The last step was to build a recommendation system capable of offering each user the job offers that best suit their profile and interests.
